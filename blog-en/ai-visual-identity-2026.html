Hands-On: Build Your AI Brand-Visual Workflow

Step 1: Define your visual DNA

Use AI to generate a master style image with your color palette, lighting and texture. In Midjourney, use --sref [master image URL] to keep your social posts and website images consistent afterward.

Step 2: Vectorize and ship commercially

AI-generated icons need to be turned into vector files. We recommend Vectorizer.ai, which converts PNG to SVG in one click — ready for print.

Three Red Lines for Commercial Use

  • Copyright ownership: use a paid subscription tier, and we recommend a second round of design on top of the AI image for legal protection.
  • Likeness rights: prefer AI-generated virtual models to avoid infringement risk with real celebrities.
  • Resolution: commercial printing needs 300 DPI. After generating, use a tool for lossless AI upscaling.
